Qingnan Fan is a Senior Researcher in the Visual Computing Group from the Tencent AI Lab. He received his Ph.D. degree from Shandong University in 2019, supervised by Prof. Baoquan Chen. He worked with Prof. Leonidas Guibas as a Postdoc Scholar at Stanford University from 2019 to 2021. His research focus lies in 3D vision, robotics, and human-computer interaction. He has published around 20 papers on the top-tier conferences and journals in the computer vision and graphics communities, including 3 CVPR/ICCV oral papers, and multiple TOG/TPAMI papers.
